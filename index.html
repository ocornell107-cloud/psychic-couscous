Common questions

Everything you want to know.

Does the AI actually sound human?
+
Yes — and this is something we're obsessive about. We use premium voice synthesis that's warm, natural, and professional. Most callers don't realize they're speaking with an AI. They simply notice how fast and helpful the experience is. You'll hear it yourself in the free demo call before we ever ask you to commit.
What if a patient asks something the AI doesn't know?
+
We train your AI specifically on your practice — your services, policies, FAQs, pricing, and team. For anything genuinely outside scope, it gracefully takes a message and flags it for your team immediately. Nothing falls through the cracks.
How does it connect to my existing schedule?
+
We integrate with the most widely-used practice management and scheduling systems. Our team handles the entire connection during onboarding — your existing workflow doesn't change at all. Patients book directly into your real calendar.
Is there a long-term contract?
+
No. All plans are month-to-month. No setup fees, no cancellation penalties. We earn your business every month because the system works — not because you're locked in. Annual billing is available at a 17% discount.
What's the difference between Aria and Nova?
+
Aria is purpose-built for dental practices — she understands cleanings, crowns, insurance verification, x-rays, and dental emergencies. Nova is built for med spas — she knows Botox consultations, filler pricing, treatment packages, and high-touch aesthetic client expectations. We always match you to the right one.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
+
None whatsoever. You fill out a form, join a 30-minute review call, and say go. Every technical piece — the phone number, integrations, workflows, AI training — is built and managed entirely by our team. Your only job is to run your practice.
Can I hear the AI before committing?
+
Absolutely. Book a free demo and we'll walk you through a live call simulation — before you ever pay a dollar. We want you to be genuinely excited about this, not just hopeful.
